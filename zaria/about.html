About Us

Engineering
Built on Trust

Zarira Engineering Solutions Limited is a Lusaka-based multi-service engineering firm serving residential, commercial, and industrial clients across Zambia since our founding.

From foundation to full-service engineering partner

Zarira Engineering Solutions was established to fill a gap in Zambia's engineering services market — a single, integrated partner that handles civil construction, electrical and mechanical engineering, equipment commissioning, consulting, procurement, logistics, and car hire under one management team.

Today we serve a diverse client base spanning private homeowners, commercial developers, industrial facilities, and institutional clients. Our team combines technical certification with on-the-ground experience across Lusaka and Zambia's provinces.
